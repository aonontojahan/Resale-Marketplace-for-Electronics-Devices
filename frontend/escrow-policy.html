🔐 How Our Escrow System Works

ReSale's escrow ensures that no money changes hands until both parties are satisfied. We act as a trusted neutral third party holding funds in a secure digital wallet.

1

Buyer Initiates Payment

The buyer deposits the full purchase amount (plus the 2.5% service fee) into the ReSale Escrow Wallet. The funds are held securely and neither the seller nor ReSale staff can access them at this point.

💳 Accepted: bKash, Nagad, Rocket, Bank Transfer, Debit/Credit Card
2

Seller Is Notified & Ships

Once funds are confirmed in escrow, the seller receives an automated notification with the buyer's delivery details. The seller must ship or hand over the device within 3 business days.

📦 Seller must upload a tracking ID or meetup confirmation within 3 days
3

Buyer Receives & Inspects

The buyer has a 7-day inspection window starting from the moment of receipt. During this time, the buyer should thoroughly inspect the device — check IMEI, battery health, screen, and test all functions.

🔍 Check: IMEI/Serial number, Battery Health, Screen integrity, iCloud/Google lock status
4

Release or Dispute

If the buyer is satisfied, they click "Release Funds" and the seller receives payment immediately minus the 2.5% fee. If there's a problem, the buyer opens a dispute within the 7-day window and our Trust Team steps in.

✅ Release → Seller paid instantly  |  ⚠️ Dispute → Trust Team reviews within 48h

🛒 Buyer Protection Policy

As a buyer on ReSale, you are protected at every step. We guarantee the following:

📅

7-Day Inspection Window

From the moment you receive the device, you have a full 7 days to inspect it. If anything doesn't match the listing description, raise a dispute — no questions asked.

💵

Full Refund Guarantee

If your dispute is upheld by our Trust Team, you receive a 100% refund of the purchase amount back to your original payment method within 3–5 business days.

🔒

Funds Never Leave Escrow Early

We never release your funds to the seller until you explicitly confirm satisfaction or the 7-day window lapses without a dispute. Your money is always safe.

🚫

Blocked Malicious Sellers

Any seller who misrepresents their device, sends a wrong item, or fails to ship is permanently banned from the platform and liable for a full refund.

⚠️ Important: Buyer protection only applies to transactions completed through the ReSale Escrow system. Deals made outside the platform (cash, direct transfer) are not covered. Always use Escrow.

📦 Seller Protection Policy

Sellers are equally protected. We make sure legitimate sellers always get paid for honest, accurate listings.

✅

Funds Guaranteed Before You Ship

You will never be asked to ship your device before the buyer's funds are confirmed in escrow. No more ghosting buyers — if the money is in escrow, you ship.

⚡

Instant Payout on Release

As soon as the buyer releases funds or the 7-day window closes without a dispute, your earnings (minus the 2.5% fee) are instantly credited to your ReSale wallet.

🛡️

Fraudulent Buyer Protection

If a buyer attempts to falsely claim a device was "not as described" without proper evidence, our Trust Team will review evidence from both sides before any refund is issued.

📋

Transparent Listing Process

All listings go through an admin review before being publicly visible — this means your listing is vetted, which builds buyer trust and increases your sale chances.

📌 Seller Responsibility: Listings must be accurate and honest. Misrepresenting condition, specs, or accessories will result in immediate listing removal and account suspension.

⚖️ Dispute Resolution

Disputes are handled by our neutral Trust Team, who reviews evidence from both parties and makes a final decision within 48 hours.

01

File a Dispute

Buyer opens a dispute within the 7-day inspection window from their wallet page. A reason and supporting evidence (photos, screenshots) must be provided.

02

Seller Responds

Seller has 24 hours to respond with counter-evidence — original photos, tracking info, original listing details, and communication history.

03

Trust Team Reviews

Our Trust Team reviews all evidence objectively. We may contact both parties for additional information if the case is unclear.

04

Decision & Action (48h)

A final decision is issued within 48 hours. If upheld, buyer receives a full refund. If denied, funds are released to the seller. Both parties are notified.

🔏 Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Here's exactly what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it.

What We Collect

  • Account Data: Full name, email address, and hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords).
  • Transaction Data: Purchase amounts, listing details, and escrow history — used to process payments and resolve disputes.
  • Communication Logs: In-platform chat messages between buyers and sellers for dispute resolution purposes.
  • Device Information: Browser type and IP address for security and fraud prevention.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect or store full payment card numbers.
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties.
  • We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

Data Retention

Account data is retained for as long as your account is active. Transaction records are retained for 2 years for legal and financial compliance. You may request deletion of your account data by contacting our support team.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, email us at aonontojahan@gmail.com.
