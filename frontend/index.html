Safety Tips

📍

Meeting Point

Always meet in a public, well-lit place like a mall or cafe. Never go to a private residence alone.

🔍

Inspect Device

Thoroughly check the device (IMEI, battery health, screens) before releasing funds from Escrow.

💳

Secure Payment

Always use the ReSale Escrow system. Avoid cash or direct bank transfers outside the platform.

📞

Verification

Only deal with verified users with good ratings and previous successful transactions.

Tradewise Safety Guard

📍

Public Meeting

Always choose established public hubs like Bashundhara City or Jamuna Future Park for meetups. Safety first!

🔍

Technical Audit

Check the internal health—run benchmark tests and check for iCloud/Google locks before the final handshake.

💳

No Direct Cash

Never pay cash directly. Use our Escrow payment to ensure your money remains refundable until you're happy.
