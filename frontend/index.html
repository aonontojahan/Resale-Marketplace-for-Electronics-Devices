How Our Escrow System Works

Every transaction on ReSale is protected by our intelligent escrow engine — your money is always safe.

💳
01

Buyer Deposits

Buyer sends payment to the ReSale Escrow wallet. Funds are locked — seller cannot touch them yet.

→
📦
02

Seller Ships

Seller sees funds are secured and ships the device. A tracking ID is submitted to the platform.

→
🔍
03

Buyer Inspects

Buyer has a 7-day inspection window to verify condition, IMEI, and functionality of the device.

→
✅
04

Funds Released

Buyer confirms satisfaction. Escrow releases funds immediately to the seller's wallet.

🛡️
7-Day Buyer Protection

Raise a dispute within 7 days of receiving the item for a full refund.

⚖️
Neutral Dispute Resolution

Our trust team reviews evidence from both parties and resolves within 48 hours.

💰
2.5% Escrow Service Fee

A small fee paid by the seller upon successful transaction completion only.
